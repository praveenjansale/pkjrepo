Kundapur

Kundapur, also spelt as Kundaapra, Kundapura, Coondapur is a coastal town in the Indian state of Karnataka. It is administered by the Kundapur Town Municipal Council and serves as the headquarters of the Kundapura Taluk of Udupi district.
